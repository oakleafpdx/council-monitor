**Who raised it:** Prosper Portland staff presented the programs, with extensive public testimony from business owners, workforce organizations, and community partners supporting continued funding.

**Decisions/Next steps:** Budget deliberations will continue with final adoption scheduled for May 19th. Multiple speakers urged restoration of proposed cuts to workforce development and small business programs.

**Relevance:** Significant for multifamily developers as Prosper becomes more active in middle-income housing acquisition and development, potentially as both partner and competitor in the market.
